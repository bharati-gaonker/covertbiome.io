Research Direction

Building a computational intelligence layer for disease biology.

Our work focuses on reverse-engineering drug–disease biology by integrating public and private biomedical signals into structured, research-ready systems.

01

Disease Mechanisms

Map biological pathways, genes, phenotypes, and molecular signals associated with disease progression and response.

02

Drug Relationships

Connect drugs, targets, mechanisms, clinical evidence, and biological context to identify therapeutic relationships.

03

Hidden Signals

Detect patterns across fragmented datasets that may reveal new hypotheses for research and discovery.

Platform Concept

From fragmented biomedical data to mechanistic insight.

The platform is designed as a research system combining data harmonization, biological knowledge graphs, machine learning, and foundation-model style representation learning.
